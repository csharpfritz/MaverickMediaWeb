History

Maverick Media began in the mid 1980's when Maverick Photography was born. For the past 25 plus years, the Principal - Linda J. McLin - has been a successful Photographer. Her business, Maverick Photography, has resided both in Tennessee and North Carolina over the years. As the economy and client needs have become more dynamic, she has broadened her horizons and branched into Web Development.

As a result of her extensive career experience, she also has the skills to offer her customers Project Management solutions, Digital Imaging and Graphical services, and most specialized, Consultation Services. Please see the appropriate pages to answer your additional questions about these issues
